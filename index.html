A LITTLE ABOUT US

What is the Art of Living all about?

Operating in 180 countries, The Art of Living Foundation is a non-profit, volunteer-based organization founded in 1981 by the humanitarian and spiritual teacher Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar. We offer numerous highly effective self-development programs that facilitate the elimination of stress and foster profound inner peace, happiness and well-being for all.
